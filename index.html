Mission/Vision

Enable the Pandora box of citizen science with climate data.

What is BaumSchutz?

BaumSchutz is an innovative tool designed to enhance and protect the ecosystem services provided by urban trees. It empowers communities and professionals to monitor and improve the health and sustainability of urban forests.

How?

By combining local data sources, high-resolution remote sensing products, and citizen science to deliver actionable insights and promote active participation in urban tree management.

Who is it for?

  • Kids: Learn about environmental stewardship while engaging in fun, educational activities.
  • Urban Tree Managers and Rangers: BaumSchutz provides comprehensive reports on the health status of parks and individual trees, enabling data-driven decision-making.
