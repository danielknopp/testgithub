Mission/Vision

Enable the Pandora box of citizen science with climate data.

What is BaumSchutz?

BaumSchutz is an innovative tool designed to enhance and protect the ecosystem services provided by urban trees...

How?

By combining local data sources, high-resolution remote sensing products, and citizen science...

Who is it for?

  • Kids: Learn about environmental stewardship through gamification.
  • Urban Tree Managers: Comprehensive reports on the health of urban forests.
